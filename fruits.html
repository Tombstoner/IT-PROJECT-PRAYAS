Universally known fruits .

Apples

Apples are a popular fruit, containing antioxidants, vitamins, dietary fiber, and a range of other nutrients.

Lemons

The lemon (Citrus limon) is a species of small evergreen trees in the flowering plant family Rutaceae, native to Asia, primarily Northeast India (Assam), Northern Myanmar or China.

Banana

Bananas are one of the most popular fruits worldwide. They contain essential nutrients that can have a protective impact on health.

Grapes

A grape is a fruit, botanically a berry, of the deciduous woody vines of the flowering plant genus Vitis.
